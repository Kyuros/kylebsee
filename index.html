Who am I?

Currently a Ph.D. Candidate

I am a 5th year in the University of Florida J. Crayton Pruitt Family Department of Biomedical Engineering program studying machine learning and neuroscience.

My current work revolves around generative AI and its uses on mapping non-contrast computed tomography (NCCT) and computed tomography (CT) perfusion maps.

I also enjoy visualizing my work with illustrative programs like Adobe Illustrator and creating tutorials with an emphasis on visualizing concepts.
